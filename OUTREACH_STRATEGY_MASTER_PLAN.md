### **Call Script - Warm Leads (Quoted Prospects):**

**Opening (15 seconds):**
```
"Hi [Name], this is Basyir from ASTA. How are you?

[Wait for response]

I'm calling to follow up on the quotation we sent for [Product/Service] for [Company]. Do you have about 10 minutes to discuss?"

[If yes, continue. If no, schedule callback]
```

**Discovery (2-3 minutes):**
```
"Great! Let me start by asking:

1. Have you had a chance to review our proposal?
2. What did you think about the [specific feature] we proposed?
3. Are there any questions or concerns I can address?
4. What's your timeline for making a decision on this?
5. Is there anyone else involved in this decision that I should speak with?"
```

**Handling Objections:**

**"Too expensive"**
```
"I understand budget is always a concern. Can I ask - what were you expecting in terms of investment? 

[Listen]

Let me show you the ROI: [Similar Company] recovered their investment in [X months] through [specific benefits]. Would it help if I broke down the cost-benefit analysis?"
```

**"Need to think about it"**
```
"Absolutely, this is an important decision. To help you think it through, what specific aspects are you considering? Is it:
- The implementation timeline?
- The technical integration?
- The budget approval?
- Something else?

[Listen and address specific concern]

Would it help if I sent you [case study/additional info] on that specific point?"
```

**"Already have a solution"**
```
"That's great that you have something in place! Can I ask - how is it working for you? Any gaps or frustrations?

[Listen]

Many of our customers actually came from [Competitor], and what they found valuable was [unique benefit]. Would you be open to seeing a quick comparison?"
```

**Closing (1-2 minutes):**
```
"Based on our conversation, it sounds like [summarize their needs]. 

I recommend we:
1. [Next step 1]
2. [Next step 2]
3. [Timeline/decision date]

Does that sound good to you?

[Get commitment]

Perfect! I'll send you a confirmation email within the hour. Can I check - what's the best email for you?"
```

**Recording Reminder:**
```
"Before we finish, just to confirm - this call has been recorded for quality and training purposes. Is that okay with you?"

[Wait for confirmation]

"Great! Thanks for your time today, [Name]. Talk soon!"
```

## üéØ **Best Practices by Channel**

### **WhatsApp Best Practices:**

**DO:**
‚úÖ Send messages during business hours (9 AM - 6 PM)
‚úÖ Keep initial messages short (2-3 sentences)
‚úÖ Use voice messages for warmth (30-45 seconds max)
‚úÖ Share visual content (product images, videos)
‚úÖ Respect "Do Not Disturb" mode
‚úÖ Wait for reply before sending more
‚úÖ Use emojis sparingly (professional context)
‚úÖ Save important conversations as notes

**DON'T:**
‚ùå Send multiple messages without response
‚ùå Message on weekends (unless they're in hospitality)
‚ùå Use WhatsApp Business broadcast (feels spammy)
‚ùå Send large files without asking
‚ùå Call without text first (except VIPs)
‚ùå Use aggressive sales language

**Recording WhatsApp Calls:**
- Inform at start of call
- Use: WhatsApp + phone recording backup
- Save to: Leads folder, tag with company name
- Transcribe key points to CRM within 24 hours

---

### **Email Best Practices:**

**DO:**
‚úÖ Personalize subject lines (company name, pain point)
‚úÖ Keep emails short (3-4 paragraphs max)
‚úÖ Use bullet points for benefits
‚úÖ Include clear CTA (call to action)
‚úÖ Add email signature with all contact methods
‚úÖ Follow up if opened but no reply
‚úÖ A/B test subject lines
‚úÖ Send during business hours

**DON'T:**
‚ùå Use generic "Dear Sir/Madam"
‚ùå Send attachments in first email (unless requested)
‚ùå Write long essays (people skim)
‚ùå Use all caps or multiple exclamation marks
‚ùå Send from generic email addresses
‚ùå Forget to proofread

**Email Timing:**
- **Best days:** Tuesday-Thursday

- **Action:** Move to long-term nurture list
- **Update CRM:** Set reminder for 6 months

- Listen fully without interrupting
- Acknowledge: "I understand your concern about [X]"
- Address with evidence: Case study, data, testimonial
- **Action:** Send supporting materials
- **Update CRM:** Note objection and how addressed

- Touch 2: Wait 5 days, different channel
- Touch 3: Wait 7 days, final attempt
- After 3 attempts: Move to quarterly nurture
- **Update CRM:** Mark as "Non-responsive - Nurture"
