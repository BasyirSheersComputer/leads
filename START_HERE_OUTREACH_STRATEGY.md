## üí° **Quick Tips:**

### **WhatsApp:**
- Send in batches of 20 (then wait for responses)
- Best time: 10 AM - 12 PM Malaysia time
- Voice messages: Only after text got no response
- Keep messages short (2-3 sentences)

### **Email:**
- Personalize subject line
- Keep body under 150 words
- One clear call-to-action
- Follow up if opened but no reply (Day 4)

### **Phone:**
- Call after WhatsApp/email engagement
- Have script visible but don't read it
- Record every call (with permission)
- Send follow-up email within 1 hour
