## ðŸ’¡ PRO TIPS

### **WhatsApp Pro Tips:**
1. **Save contacts first** - Add to phone with company name
2. **Profile picture matters** - Professional photo of yourself
3. **Status updates** - Can put company info in WhatsApp status
4. **Group similar leads** - Create WhatsApp broadcast lists (up to 256)
5. **Voice > Text** - For important conversations
6. **Timing** - Morning messages get better response
7. **Read receipts** - If they read but don't reply, wait 1 day then send voice
8. **Images work** - Product photos get 3x engagement

### **Email Pro Tips:**
1. **Personalize sender name** - "Basyir - ASTA" not just company name
2. **Mobile-friendly** - 60% open on mobile, keep it short
3. **One CTA** - Don't confuse with multiple asks
4. **P.S. works** - People read P.S., use for key point
5. **Track everything** - Use email tracking tools
6. **Time send** - Schedule for optimal times
7. **Follow-up** - If opened but no reply, follow up
8. **A/B test** - Test subject lines with small batches

### **Phone Pro Tips:**
1. **Smile while calling** - They can hear it in your voice
2. **Stand up** - More energy in your voice
3. **Script nearby** - But don't read robotically  
4. **Take notes** - Write during call
5. **Confirm next steps** - Before hanging up
6. **Send follow-up immediately** - Within 1 hour
7. **Record calls** - With permission, for training
8. **Time blocking** - Batch calls for efficiency
